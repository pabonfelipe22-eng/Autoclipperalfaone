TikTok Integration

AutoClipper integrates with TikTok's official APIs to allow creators to securely connect their accounts, generate clips, and optionally publish edited content directly to TikTok.

Secure Login

Creators authenticate using TikTok Login to securely connect their accounts.

Video Upload

Generated clips can be uploaded to TikTok using the official API.

Content Publishing

Users may publish clips directly to their TikTok accounts with their approval.

This application uses TikTok APIs with the following permissions: user.info.basic, video.upload, video.publish.
