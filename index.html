About the Platform

AutoClipper is a web platform designed to simplify the process of publishing short-form content directly to TikTok. The platform integrates with official TikTok developer tools, allowing creators to authenticate securely and upload videos directly to their TikTok accounts.

The application focuses on providing a streamlined publishing workflow while maintaining full compliance with TikTok Developer Platform policies.

TikTok Integration

  • Login Kit — Secure TikTok account authentication
  • Content Posting API — Upload and publish videos

Permissions Requested

  • user.info.basic
  • video.upload
  • video.publish

Platform Features

Upload video content directly to TikTok through the platform.
